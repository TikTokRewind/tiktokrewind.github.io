## 2. What the Channel does

TikTokRewind selects clips from public short-form video archives and produces composite posts. Each post includes:

- An excerpt of the original short-form video (typically under 60 seconds)
- An editorial "where are they now" update on the original creator
- Visible attribution to the original creator
- A TikTokRewind watermark

The Channel does not operate any user-facing website or web application. It publishes content to third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) which have their own terms of service governing your use of those platforms.

## 4. Creator opt-out

The Channel honors creator opt-out requests. If you are the original creator of a clip featured on the Channel, or if you appear in such a clip and wish for it to be removed:

1. Contact the operator at the address in section 9.
2. Identify the clip (TikTokRewind post URL, or original-creator handle plus an approximate date).
3. The operator will remove the clip from all three platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) within 7 days of verification and will not feature your content again unless you affirmatively grant permission.

Opt-out is permanent for the requesting party. The operator maintains an internal denylist to ensure removed content is not re-posted.
